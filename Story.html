The Troubled Waters

The Arctic Ocean, one of the planet's most fragile ecosystems, faces dire challenges due to climate change.

As CO2 levels rise, temperatures increase, leading to melting ice caps and threatening marine life.

This delicate balance is disrupted, and urgent action is needed to protect this vital part of our planet.
